Review

Look

The can looks pretty neat, black red and silver, with a black stylized dragon on it. Plus the name rocks pretty hard, could be a heavy metal band.

Taste

O.K. Megaforce, do not fail me, i have high hopes based on your name! Mhhh, nothing special with the smell but i still hope for the best. BAH!!! Fuck, why you fail me so hard? Nearly no CO2 inside, really really sour and has some kind of plastic taste. This me-too product fails for me at least.

Overall

This drink could be good if the taste wouldn't suck so bad, well at least it already has the right name. Megaforce is also a 1982 film, which supposendly sucks pretty bad. So the drink and the film has at least that in common. At least the price was good, even though i got it as special offer.

Rating: 4.0 of 10
