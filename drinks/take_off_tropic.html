Review

Look

The drink comes in a yellow can with a red 'start engine' button.

Taste

Take Off Tropic has nothing of the taste of an ordinary energy drink. It has the taste of maracuja spritzer.

Overall

Well, it certainly tastes somewhat 'tropic' even if it does not really contains anything more exotic than grapes and oranges (it has more taurine than maracuja juice). Still this drink is not too shabby. The can is also quite big.

Rating: 7.0 of 10
