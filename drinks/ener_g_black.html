Review

Look

Black Can with red and bronze face. It says High energy and Cola Drink at the front.

Taste

At first, it is really really sweet. It should taste like Cola but it sure tastes like everything BUT cola. The taste is something between berry juice,citrus and Gummy bear taste. It somewhat reminds me of the Coke of Red bull, which does not taste good either.

Overall

Besides being too frickin sweet for my taste it is not really bad. It has a below average caffein content and far below average taurine content. It is not really my taste, check it out anyway if you get the chance, because the price is actually really cheap.

Rating: 5.5 of 10
