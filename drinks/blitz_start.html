Review

Look

Small red bottle or vial with Michael Schumacher on it. Looks similar to other shots like Red Bull Shot.

Taste

Smells really intense like multi vitamin drinks. The first sip is extremely sour, i guess you have to drink it at once. Oh my god it tastes so weird... Like very sour syrup or something. I dont know.

Overall

Well what can i say, this was my first SHOT and i cannot really say i like the taste. It contains a vast amount of added vitamins and stuff, so it has to be good. The vial is very small and perfect to-go for the quick energy shot. It costs a lot less like the Red Bull Shots here. At least try it once.

Rating: 6.7 of 10
