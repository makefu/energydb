Review

Look

A brown see-through bottle with a silver and red label. The label has an eagle at the front.

Taste

Smells really intense. Very few CO2, and a very strong gummy bear taste. It also is very sweet. After taste is somewhat sour.

Overall

I am a bit disappointed by the drink because it provides not the usual quality expected from Aldi. Its incredibly cheap though.At least give it a try if you see it. It has a too intense taste and not enough CO2 for what i like.

Rating: 5.2 of 10
